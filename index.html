The founder

Built by a local,
for the long term.

Derek LeBon founded BONLIFE Capital after a decade closing residential and investment loans across coastal California. He noticed something simple: the wealthier the client, the more cookie-cutter the experience often felt.

BONLIFE was built to be the opposite of that — a boutique advisory where every loan is shaped to the client, the property, and the long arc of the portfolio. No call centers. No 800-numbers. Just one direct line, from first conversation through final wire.
